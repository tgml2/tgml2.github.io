Product design and prototyping course

TGML2 is a product design and prototyping course funded by the Development Innovations project from USAID. 20 Cambodian students will study 2d, 3d, mechanical and electronic design, and create their own products in teams over 2 months, pitching their work to a panel of local and international experts and business leaders. 2 teams will be selected to receive professional product design support from the course organizer Bespokh, who offer design, innovation and manufacturing consultancy.
